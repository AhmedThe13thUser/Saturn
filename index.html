    About Saturn

    Saturn is a newly formed FIRST Tech Challenge team based in Albayda, Libya. This is our inaugural season, and we're excited to embark on this incredible journey of innovation, teamwork, and technological exploration.

    Our mission is to inspire young minds through hands-on STEM education, fostering creativity, problem-solving skills, and collaboration. We believe that robotics is not just about building machines, but about building character, confidence, and community.
